FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need my own servers?▾
Yes — that's the point. You practice on your own cloud VMs (AWS, GCP, Azure) or local VMs. We provide a free VM setup guide for each provider.
Is there a browser terminal?▾
Not right now. Working on your own machine builds skills that transfer directly to real infrastructure. A managed lab environment may come later.
Which topics are covered?▾
Linux administration, Docker, Kubernetes (CKA-aligned), Ansible, CI/CD with GitHub Actions, and Terraform — with more tracks on the way.
Is it good for certification prep?▾
Absolutely. The Kubernetes and Linux tracks map to CKA and LFCS objectives, so hands-on practice doubles as exam preparation.
